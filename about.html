About Us

We're builders.
Not a branding exercise.

Dremora IT is a technology studio founded in Pune, India. We started because we believed there was a gap between what clients actually need and what most agencies deliver — which is often an over-designed website and an under-built product.

We are not trying to look like a company with 200 employees and 500 clients. We are a small, focused team that builds software carefully, communicates clearly, and stays involved long after launch.

Our Story

Dremora started with a straightforward observation: most small and medium businesses in India either can't afford good software, or they end up with something that looks impressive but doesn't actually solve their problem.

We're building a studio that does the opposite. We ask what you actually need before we quote you anything. We explain what we're building before we build it. We write code that you can understand and maintain. And when we deliver, we don't disappear.

That's the whole pitch. It's not revolutionary. But from what we've seen, it's rarer than it should be.

How We Work

What we believe

These are the principles that guide every project and every conversation we have.

01

Honesty over salesmanship

We'll tell you when a project isn't right for us, when your timeline is unrealistic, or when a simpler solution would serve you better. We'd rather lose a deal than overpromise and underdeliver.

02

Working software over meetings

We prefer to show progress than to talk about it. You'll see working versions early and often — not just slide decks and status updates.

03

Code quality matters

We write code that other developers can read, maintain, and extend. Software built badly costs more in the long run — and we've seen the damage that creates for businesses.

04

Long-term thinking

We're not trying to close one project and move on. We want clients who come back to us as their business grows — and who refer us because we made their life easier.

05

Context is everything

The best solution depends on your budget, your technical team, your users, and your timeline. We don't have a one-size-fits-all approach — and we're skeptical of those who do.

06

Continuous learning

Technology changes fast. We stay current with new tools, frameworks, and AI developments — not as buzzwords, but as genuine improvements to how we build.

Want to work with us?

The best way to see if we're a good fit is a 30-minute call. We'll understand your project, be direct about our capabilities, and figure out whether this makes sense for both sides.
