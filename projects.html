NagarSeva Platform

A civic complaint and governance management platform built as an internal prototype to demonstrate our capability in building complex, multi-role web applications.

CITIZEN PORTAL
ADMIN DASHBOARD
01

The Challenge

Municipal governments in India often rely on paper-based complaint systems or fragmented digital tools. Citizens have no visibility into whether their complaints are being addressed, and administrators have no unified dashboard to track and assign issues efficiently.

We built NagarSeva as a prototype to demonstrate how a well-designed digital system could address this — integrating citizen-facing submission, AI-powered categorization, and administrative workflow management in one platform.

02

Our Approach

Role-based access — Citizens, field staff, and administrators each see a different interface appropriate to their role.
AI categorization — Complaints submitted by citizens are automatically categorized using Google Gemini, reducing manual admin triage time.
Status transparency — Citizens can track complaint status in real-time without needing to call or visit an office.
Analytics dashboard — Administrators can view complaint volumes, resolution rates, and staff performance at a glance.

Outcome & Key Learnings

NagarSeva demonstrates our ability to architect and deliver complex multi-role systems with AI integrations. The prototype is fully functional and serves as a reference implementation for potential government or civic technology clients.

Key learning: Designing software for non-technical end users (government staff) requires significantly more UX investment than building for developers. Simplicity is a feature, not an afterthought.
