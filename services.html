50% advance before kickoff, 50% on delivery. We accept UPI, NEFT, IMPS (India) and Wise / PayPal (International).

Web systems: 2–6 weeks. Dashboards/CRMs: 4–10 weeks. AI agents: 3–8 weeks. Timelines are agreed upfront.

What if I need changes after delivery?

Minor revisions within scope are covered. Larger scope changes are quoted separately, transparently, before work begins.
